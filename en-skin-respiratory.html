Eczema & Respiratory: What is the Link?

Many eczema patients will eventually experience asthma or allergic rhinitis. This phenomenon is known as the Atopic March.

🧬

Why does it happen?

Our immune system is a connected network. When the skin (the first line of defense) becomes sensitive, the immune system becomes hyperactive and starts affecting other organs, such as the respiratory tract.

  • Shared genetics (Filaggrin)
  • Immunoglobulin E (IgE) reactions

Key Statistics

50%

Of children with severe eczema are at risk of developing asthma later in life.
