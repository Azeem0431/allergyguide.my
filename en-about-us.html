Who Are We?

AllergyGuide.my is a digital health education platform focused on allergy management. We believe that the right information can save lives, especially in anaphylactic emergencies.

We strive to bridge the gap between complex medical data and the daily understanding of the Malaysian public.

🎯

To be the number one trusted allergy resource for families across Malaysia.

Integrity
