1 Know Your Inhaler Types

💙

Reliever

Usually Blue in color. Used during an asthma attack to quickly open up the airways.

Use Only When Needed
🧡

Preventer

Usually Brown or Orange. Reduces inflammation in the lungs for long-term control.

Must Use Every Day

Asthma Action Plan

Green Zone (Stable)

No shortness of breath, sleeping well. Continue your daily routine.

Yellow Zone (Caution)

Starting to cough or wheeze. Use your reliever inhaler and rest.

Red Zone (EMERGENCY)

Difficult to speak or walk. Call 999 or go straight to the hospital!
