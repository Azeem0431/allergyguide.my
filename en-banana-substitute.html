Natural Substitute

Mashed Banana

Mashed banana is a great egg substitute to provide moisture and a dense texture in baked goods without using eggs.

Substitution Ratio

🥚

1 Egg

=

🍌

1/2 Banana (Mashed)

*Use approximately 1/4 cup of mashed banana for every egg.

Advantages

  • ✓ Adds moisture to cakes.
  • ✓ Natural sweetener (reduces sugar need).
  • ✓ Perfect for muffins, pancakes, and cupcakes.

Disadvantages

  • ! Leaves a strong banana flavor.
  • ! Cake texture may be slightly heavier.

💡 Tips for Best Results

Use very ripe bananas (those with black spots on the skin). Ripe bananas are easier to mash until smooth and have a higher natural sugar content, making them a better binding agent.
