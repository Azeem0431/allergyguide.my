Asthma Attack First Aid

01

Sit the person upright. Do not allow them to lie down.

02

Use a reliever inhaler (Blue) — 1 puff every 30-60 seconds (up to a maximum of 10 puffs).

03

If there is no improvement within 10 minutes, call 999 immediately.
