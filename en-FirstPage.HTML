Allergy Myths vs Facts

MYTH ❌

"Allergies are just minor rashes and will go away on their own."

This statement is dangerous. Allergies can progress into severe breathing difficulties (Anaphylaxis) within minutes.

FACT ✅

"Every body has a different reaction."

Allergies can appear at any time even if you have never experienced them before.

Allergy or Intolerance?

Many people confuse food allergies with food intolerances (such as lactose intolerance).

  • ⚠️ Allergy: Involves the immune system (Dangerous).
  • 🤢 Intolerance: Involves the digestive system (Stomach ache).

"Knowing the difference can save your life."

Did You Know? (Cross-Reactivity)

"If you are allergic to cow's milk, you have a 90% chance of being allergic to goat's milk."

This is known as 'Cross-Reactivity,' where proteins from different substances are identified as the same by your immune system.

Allergy-Free Zone at Home

Transform your living space into a healthier and safer environment.

🛏️

Bedroom

Wash bedsheets weekly in 60°C hot water to kill dust mites.

Tips for Dining Out

✅ Ask About Ingredients: Don't be shy to ask if sauces contain shrimp paste (belacan) or nuts.

✅ Watch the Oil: Was the oil used to fry shrimp also used for your chicken?

✅ Bring an Allergy Card: Show a written note to your waiter.

How Do Allergies Occur?

🦠

1. Exposure

Foreign substances (allergens) enter the body through air, contact, or food.

🛡️

2. IgE Antibodies

The immune system mistakenly identifies it as a threat and produces IgE antibodies.

💥

3. Histamine Release

Cells release chemicals (Histamine) that cause itching, swelling, and sneezing.

Allergies at the Workplace?

Certain occupations have a high risk of exposure to industrial allergens.

🩺 Healthcare

Latex glove allergies.

👩‍🍳 Bakery

Asthma caused by flour dust.

How Do Doctors Confirm Allergies?

Skin Prick Test

Drops of allergens are placed on the skin and pricked slightly to see if a rash or swelling reaction occurs within 15-20 minutes.

Blood Test (IgE)

A blood sample is taken to measure the amount of specific antibodies (Immunoglobulin E) produced by your immune system.

Oral Food Challenge

The most accurate test where patients consume small amounts of suspected food under strict medical supervision in a hospital.

*Note: Please consult an allergist or immunologist for an accurate diagnosis.

Love Cats But Sneezing?

Cat allergies are actually caused by protein in the saliva (Fel d 1), not the fur.

📌 Use a vacuum with a HEPA filter.

📌 Bathe the cat at least once a week.

📌 Create a "Cat-Free Zone" (especially in the bedroom).
