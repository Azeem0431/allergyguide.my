Mite-Free Zone

Revolutionize your cleaning routine to reduce allergy flare-ups by up to 70%.

🛏️ Focus: The Bedroom

Wash Temperature

Wash bedsheets and pillowcases in water at least 60°C to completely kill dust mites.

Allergen Proofing

Use anti-dust mite covers for mattresses and pillows to create a physical barrier against mites.

🚫 Don't Make These Mistakes!

🧹
Dry Sweeping

Traditional brooms simply kick dust into the air. Use a damp mop or a vacuum equipped with a HEPA filter instead.

🧸
Neglecting Soft Toys

Plush toys are dust magnets. Pro-tip: Place them in a sealed bag and put them in the freezer for 24 hours to kill mites.
