    Allergy Categories

    Explore various types of allergies, understand their triggers, and learn how to manage symptoms effectively.

    Most Common

    Food Allergy

    Immune system reactions to specific food proteins such as peanuts, seafood, and milk.

    Dermatology

    Skin Allergy

    Conditions like Eczema and Hives that cause itching, rashes, and skin inflammation.

    Respiratory

    Respiratory Allergy

    Allergies to dust, pollen, or mold that affect the airways, nose, and sinuses.
