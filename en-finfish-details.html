Protein Allergen

Finfish

Finfish allergy includes fish like Mackerel, Sea Bass, Salmon, and Pomfret. Fish proteins are highly heat-stable, so cooking does not reduce the risk of an allergic reaction.

1 Examples of Finfish

Mackerel (Kembung)
Sea Bass (Siakap)
Pomfret (Bawal)
Tuna
Salmon
Cod

Important Info: Histamine Poisoning

Sometimes, a reaction after eating fish is not caused by an allergy, but by **Scombroid Poisoning**. This occurs when fish that isn't fresh produces high levels of histamine. Symptoms are similar to an allergy, but the root cause is the freshness of the fish.
