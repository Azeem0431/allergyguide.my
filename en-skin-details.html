Skin Allergy

An inflammatory reaction when the skin comes into contact with external substances or is triggered by the immune system from within the body.

Emergency Signs Treatment Tips

1 Skin Allergy Classification

🧴

Contact Dermatitis

Occurs when skin comes into direct contact with substances like nickel, perfumes, or soaps.

❄️

Hives (Gegata)

Raised red, itchy bumps. Appears suddenly due to food, medication, or stress.

🧬

Atopic Eczema

A chronic condition of dry, itchy skin lasting for a long time, often linked to genetics.

🐝

Angioedema

Swelling under the skin layers, usually around the eyes or lips. Requires immediate treatment.

Popular Triggers in Malaysia

🧺 Detergents 🐱 Pet Dander 🍤 Seafood ☀️ Hot Weather

2 Initial Steps at Home

🧊
Cold Compress

Use a cold towel to reduce itching and swelling on the skin.

🧴
Calamine Lotion

Apply cooling lotion to soothe red skin inflammation.

⚠️ Warning:
  • Do not scratch the allergic area to prevent skin infections.
  • Use fragrance-free and hypoallergenic body soap.
  • Avoid hot showers as they can worsen the itching.

Professional Allergy Testing

Get accurate confirmation through dermatological testing at hospitals or specialist clinics.

🩸
Skin Prick Test

Skin prick test to detect allergies to dust, fur, or food.

🩹
Patch Test

Patch placed on the back to detect chemical/cosmetic allergies.

🔬
Blood Test (IgE)

Blood test to measure immune system antibody levels against allergens.
