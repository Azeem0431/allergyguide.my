Natural Leavening Agent

Soda & Vinegar

This mixture produces carbon dioxide which makes your cake rise and become very fluffy without requiring eggs as a leavening agent.

Replacement Ratio

🥚

1 Egg

=

1 Teaspoon

Baking Soda

+

1 Tablespoon

Apple/White Vinegar

1 How to Use

Step 1

Mix the dry ingredients (including baking soda) and wet ingredients separately.

Step 2

Add the vinegar into the wet ingredients, then combine both mixtures and immediately put it into the oven.

Best For:

  • • Chocolate Cake (most effective)
  • • Sponge / Vanilla Cake
  • • Light Muffins
  • • Quick breads

Important Note

You will not taste the vinegar in the final result because it is neutralized by the baking soda during the baking process.
