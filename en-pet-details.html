🔬 Identify the Real Cause

Cat allergies stem from the Fel d 1 protein, while dog allergies come from the Can f 1 protein. These proteins are found in:

👅 Saliva
🧪 Urine
🧴 Oil Glands
🏠 Dander (Dead Skin)

Myth vs. Fact

Myth

"Hairless cats (Sphynx) do not cause allergies."

Fact

Incorrect. Even without fur, Sphynx cats still produce allergen proteins through their skin and saliva.

Myth

"Washing your pet daily will eliminate allergies."

Fact

Washing temporarily reduces dander, but allergen levels typically return to normal within just 2-3 days.
