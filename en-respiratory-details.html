Understanding Respiratory Allergies

A respiratory allergy is more than just a common cold. It is an immune response to microscopic particles that interferes with your quality of life and sleep.

1 Main Triggers in Malaysia

🌫️

House Dust Mites

#1 Cause in Malaysia

Thrives in pillows, mattresses, and fabric sofas that are damp (Humidity > 60%).

🐈

Pet Dander

Cats & Dogs

It's not the fur, but proteins in dead skin (dander), saliva, and pet urine.

🍄

Mold & Spores

Bathroom & Aircond Issues

Grows on damp walls and air conditioning systems that are not serviced regularly.

🪳

Cockroach Allergens

Proteins in cockroach droppings, saliva, and body parts are major asthma triggers in urban areas.

🏭

Haze & PM2.5

Fine particles from open burning that seep into the bloodstream and worsen eczema and breathing.

☀️

Sweat & Heat

Malaysia's humid weather traps sweat, triggering heat rashes and extreme itchiness.

2 Medical Treatment Options

Oral Antihistamines

EXAMPLES: Zyrtec, Clarityne, Telfast

Quick solution for itching and sneezing. Choose 'Second Generation' (Loratadine/Cetirizine) to avoid drowsiness during work hours.

Nasal Corticosteroids

EXAMPLES: Nasonex, Flixonase, Avamys

Long-term treatment for chronic nasal congestion. Unlike 'Decongestants', these treat the underlying immune system inflammation.

Steroid Creams / TCI

EXAMPLES: Hydrocortisone, Elomet, Protopic

Used to relieve eczema redness. Must be used according to doctor's instructions to prevent skin thinning.

LTRA (Chewable)

EXAMPLE: Singulair (Montelukast)

Highly effective for patients with a combination of Allergic Rhinitis and Asthma simultaneously.

Eye Drops

EXAMPLES: Patanol, Olopatadine

Quickly relieves itching, redness, and swelling caused by allergic conjunctivitis.

Moisturizers (Emollients)

EXAMPLES: Cetaphil, QV, Ceradan

Not just regular lotion. It repairs the skin barrier damaged by allergic attacks.

💡

Important Note: Avoid overusing 'Decongestant Nasal Sprays' (like Otrivin) for more than 5 consecutive days to prevent "Rebound Congestion."

Allergy Myths vs Facts

❌

MYTH: "Drug/food allergies disappear as we grow up."

FACT: Some allergies (like nuts or medications) are usually lifelong. In fact, new allergies can suddenly appear in adulthood.

✅

FACT: "Allergies can cause mental stress."

Sleep disturbances due to chronic itching can lead to anxiety and depression. Allergy treatment is not just for the skin, but for quality of life.

3 Medical Treatment Archive

💊

Antihistamine Generations

GEN-1 (DROWSY)

Chlorpheniramine (Piriton). Effective but causes strong drowsiness. Best for nighttime use.

GEN-2 (NON-DROWSY)

Loratadine, Cetirizine, Bilastine. The gold standard for daily use without cognitive side effects.

🧴

Topical & Sprays

Nasal Steroids (Avamys/Nasonex)

The most effective treatment for sinusitis. Works by reducing localized swelling inside the nose.

Barrier Cream (Ceramide)

Repairs the 'leaky' skin barrier in eczema patients so allergens cannot easily enter.

⚠️ When to go to the Hospital Immediately?

Do not wait if you experience:

  • Severe shortness of breath (Asthma attack)
  • 'Wheezing' or whistling sounds while breathing
  • Bluish tint on lips or fingernails
  • Sharp or stabbing chest pain
