🍲

Ginger & Garlic Chicken Soup

Ginger acts as a natural decongestant to thin out mucus.

Quick Method:

Sauté plenty of ginger and garlic with a bit of olive oil. Add chicken and carrots. Boil until tender.

🍹

"Green Relief" Juice

A blend of green vegetables to neutralize acid and inflammation.

Ingredients:

Celery, green apple, and a squeeze of lemon. Blend and drink fresh in the morning.

🥩

Turmeric Grilled Salmon

The most powerful combination of Omega-3 and Curcumin to fight asthma.

Ingredients:

Coat salmon with fresh turmeric powder, black pepper, and a pinch of rock salt. Grill for 10 minutes.

🥗

Pumpkin Seed Salad (Magnesium)

Magnesium helps relax the muscles in the respiratory tract.

Tips:

Sprinkle roasted pumpkin seeds over your green salad or rice every day.

🍎

Apple & Almond Butter

Apples contain Quercetin which inhibits the release of histamine.

Slice apples and spread with natural almond butter (no added sugar). The safest snack for children with asthma.

🍵

Ginger Nabeez (Detox Water)

Cleanses the gut and strengthens antibodies.

Soak dates and ginger slices in plain water overnight. Drink in the morning before eating.

⚠️ "Flare-Up" Taboos

Reduce/Avoid:

  • • Canned foods (High preservatives)
  • • Soy Sauce & Sauces (High histamine fermentation)
  • • Prawns & Shellfish (If skin sensitive)
  • • Carbonated Drinks (Causes bloating & chest tightness)

"Every time you eat, notice if there is an itchy throat or sneezing within 30 minutes. Record in your food diary to identify your unique triggers."
