Elimination Diet Guide (6 Weeks)

1
Weeks 1-2: Clear Out

Stop all suspected trigger foods. Focus on fresh whole foods (Rice, Vegetables, Fresh Meat).

2
Weeks 3-4: Monitor

Observe your skin. Has the redness decreased? If yes, you are on the right track.

3
Weeks 5-6: Challenge

Reintroduce one allergen type. If itching returns within 24 hours, you've found your trigger!
