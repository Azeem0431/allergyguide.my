Moisturizing Agent

Applesauce

Applesauce acts as a fantastic moisturizing agent. It is perfect for baked goods that require a soft yet dense texture, such as brownies or muffins.

Substitution Ratio

🥚

1 Egg

=

🍎

1/4 Cup Applesauce

*Use the "Unsweetened" version for better flavor control.

Advantages

  • ✓ Creates a very moist and soft texture.
  • ✓ Neutral flavor (not as strong as banana).
  • ✓ Reduces cholesterol in cooking.

Baking Tips:

If you use applesauce, you might need to add a little **baking powder** (about 1/2 teaspoon) to the batter to help the cake rise a bit higher, as applesauce tends to make the batter quite heavy.

How to Prepare Yourself:

Peel, core, and chop the apples. Boil with a little water until soft, then mash or blend until smooth.

Best for: Fruit Cakes, Brownies & Pancakes
